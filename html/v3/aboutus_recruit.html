诚聘英才

  • BD商务拓展专员（全职/兼职）
    展开
    岗位职责 Job Description

    1、协助外籍总监跟踪开发体育装备业务和执行国际体育合作项目(足球培训、体育赛事、项目跟踪)；
    Assist the director develop the sport-facilities business，also trace and implement the international project cooperation（football-training、sports-events、sports-program）.

    2、依据公司的业务目标和策略，协助部门制定计划的组织、协调市场开拓、发展；
    Help to organize and make the market development plan according to the company’s target and strategy.

    3、组织国内足球俱乐部、青少年精英球队与全球著名足球俱乐部的访问、交流；
    Organize the visit and communication of the domestic football club and teenager football team and famous football club.

    4、负责开拓足球俱乐部市场业务，进行相关的宣传推广。
    Develop the business of football club and promotion.

    5、协助海外事务的接洽、沟通，保持良好商务合作关系。
    Contact oversea partners and keep good relations.

    任职要求 Job Requirements

    1、应届大学生毕业，男女不限，流利的英文口语及书面表达能力；
    Excellent English both written and speaking.

    2、外表形象佳、年轻有活力，性格开朗，有良好的沟通技巧，能够偶尔接受国外公差；
    Good communication skills with outgoing personality，and willing to learn, work actively.also could go business trip (aboard) sometimes.

    3、谈判能力突出，或有市场工作经验者优先；
    Excellent negotiation ability, candidates with marketing experience has priority.

    4、熟悉体育及足球领域知识和经验。
    Some experience and knowledge of sports way in football.

    5、市场营销、国际商务或贸易专业，中英文流利；
    Marketing, Communication, International Trade related major, Language both Chinese and English .

    6、有本地外籍留学生优先(可兼职)。
    Student from foreign nationality and live in native place is the best.
